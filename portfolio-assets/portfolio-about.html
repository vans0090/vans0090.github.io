Vanshika, commonly known as Vini Arts , online is a graphic designer, illustrator, and web developer. I am currently a student at Algonquin College and is learning a plethora of new advanced skills. Designing and finding designs in the nature and world is very interesting. It is the only thing that any designer can do the rest of their life. One of my main goals and objectives is to design a project based on the individual needs of a client. Prior to beginning the design phase of a project, a graphic designer speaks with a client to determine a project's overall goal, purpose and desired appearance. I take this as my main goal and work towards it.

Being in this industry has always been a dream and now, passion for me. From my childhood, I have always wondered the logo designs, the vector graphics and how they are made in the computer. The websites that we are so dependent nowadays, what is behind them? The apps we use everyday, why they are made in these layouts. After completing a painting course, I tried programming in my school which then became my major. When I looked into this course, I thought its the perfect combination of all the skills I have and want to polish. Apart from the illustrations and programming, I learnt photography, which also is now my favourite hobby. I've been into a lot of clicking nature pictures and posting them onto my social media which have gained much attention by all of my loved ones. Unlike the designing elements, writing and reading quotes about life and motivating things is one of my main hobby that i do almost all the time.Sketching and painting is also that hobby that I do sometimes because I have been learning and kept that as my subject from high school which I enjoy a lot. Talking about my personality skills, I am a very good listener and a team worker. I also strive to showcase my communication skills in order to squeeze out all the expectations of a client that they want from us designers. More talking and less improvisations is my key objective to be successful in this field which allows me to have long conversations with the clients and do less changes to my design.

My Three main virtues are:

Elegance

I believe elegance lies in the simplicity of the design that we make.

Intuitive

If the user is able to use your product without any difficulty, only then a designer is successful.

Perfection

The perfect your design is, the less revisions you need after launching it.
